Canvas is an extremely heavy-duty plain-woven fabric used for making sails, tents, marquees, backpacks, and other functions where sturdiness is required. It is also popularly used as a painting surface, typically stretched over a framework. It is our vision that the Chisimba canvas is creates a metaphorically strong material for chisimba that can be stretched over the framework and painted with content and functionality that is superior to any other.
